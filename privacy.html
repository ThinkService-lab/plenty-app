Privacy Policy

PlentyMeals ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use PlentyMeals at plenty-app-nine.vercel.app (the "App").

Short version: We collect your email only if you choose to sign up. We do not sell your data. Your ingredient inputs are sent to an AI to generate meal suggestions and are not stored.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide voluntarily:

  • Email address — only if you choose to join our mailing list or submit feedback with your email. This is entirely optional.
  • Feedback text — if you choose to submit written feedback through the app.
  • Ingredient inputs — the ingredients you enter are sent to the Anthropic AI API to generate meal suggestions. These inputs are not stored by PlentyMeals after the response is returned.

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address — collected temporarily by our servers for rate limiting and abuse prevention. It is not stored or logged beyond the current session.
  • Usage data — Vercel (our hosting provider) may collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalised meal suggestions using your ingredient inputs
  • To send you occasional product updates and meal ideas if you subscribed by email
  • To improve the App based on feedback you voluntarily provide
  • To prevent abuse and protect the security of our service

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell or share your data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

PlentyMeals uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Anthropic API — your ingredient inputs and dietary preferences are sent to Anthropic to generate meal suggestions. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — our hosting provider. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sheets (via Google Apps Script) — email addresses submitted through the signup form are stored in a private Google Sheet. See Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Health Information

PlentyMeals allows you to optionally select health conditions (such as diabetes or high blood pressure) to tailor meal suggestions. This information is:

  • Sent to the Anthropic API only to generate your meal suggestions
  • Not stored by PlentyMeals after your session ends
  • Never linked to your email address or any other identifying information
Medical disclaimer: PlentyMeals provides general nutritional guidance only and is not a medical device. Always consult your doctor or a registered dietitian before making dietary changes for a medical condition.

5. Data Retention

  • Email addresses — retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Feedback — retained to improve the App. You may request deletion at any time.
  • Ingredient inputs and health selections — not retained after your session.
  • IP addresses — not persistently stored beyond rate limiting purposes.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent for email communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@plenty-app.com.

7. Children's Privacy

PlentyMeals is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption on all connections and server-side API key management. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact Us
